About me

From a young age, I’ve been captivated by technology. My journey in tech began in high school, where I proudly served as an ambassador for the Information Technology vocational program during my studies at DeSoto County Career Tech West.

Professionally, I have been fortunate to build a well-rounded foundation. My career began as an IT technician with Future Electronics, providing hands-on solutions to tech issues and learning the intricacies of hardware and software management. Later, I was able to share my skills, teaching the fundamentals of web development at Davidson Elementary School in Water Valley, MS. Most recently, I spent over three years with Cellular South, further honing my skills in a dynamic and fast-paced environment.

Beyond my career, I am passionate about giving back to my community and fostering growth in others. I am constantly in pursuit of new knowledge, whether it’s exploring emerging technologies or nurturing my collection of houseplants. This pastime keeps me grounded and connected to nature and balance. I look forward to bringing my skills and enthusiasm to new challenges and collaborations.
